About

Things about us.

What is this group for?

ADL-publicsectr is a group for those in the public sector using (or interested in using) the programming language R.

We welcome people of all skill levels and experience! This is a space for learning and sharing in a postive way. We also eat cupcakes.

ADL-publicsectR has a safer spaces policy, which all members need to follow.

Who are you?

We’re a group of people working with data in the public sector in Adelaide.

The founding memebers of ADL-publicsectR are Owen Churches, Kristen Osborne, and Mel Heap.

Can I come along?

Of course!

Where do you meet?

Usually in the Adelaide CBD, at whoever’s office is kind enough to let some geeks hang out in their meeting rooms after hours. For specifics contact us.
